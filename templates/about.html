About nCrypt

nCrypt lets you create and share your own custom encryption methods for truly personal communication. Explore classic ciphers like Caesar and Base64, or design unique encryptions to keep messages private. Share your custom codes with friends and build a secure, personal language together.

nCrypt. Share. Connect.

Use Cases

  • Create Your Secret Language: Build custom encryptions and share them with friends for private, unbreakable conversations.
  • Secure Your Passwords: Safeguard your credentials if you store them locally, encrypt them with your custom method—only you can decode it!
  • Send Encrypted Messages: Use an encryption that only you and your friend know to text secretly, even in the official college group chat, making your conversations truly private and unique!
