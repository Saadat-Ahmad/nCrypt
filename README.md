# nCrypt

**nCrypt. Share. Connect.**

nCrypt is a web app that lets you create, use, and share custom encryption methods for truly personal communication. Explore classic ciphers and encodings, or design your own unique encryption to keep messages private between you and your friends.

🔗 **Live Site:** [saadatahmad.pythonanywhere.com](https://saadatahmad.pythonanywhere.com)

---

## Features

- **Custom Encryption** — Build your own encryption method and share it with friends for private, unbreakable conversations.
- **Classic Ciphers & Encodings** — A collection of ready-to-use encryption and encoding methods.
- **Share & Collaborate** — Share your custom codes with others and build a secure, personal language together.

---

## Use Cases

- **Create Your Secret Language** — Build custom encryptions and share them with friends for private conversations.
- **Secure Your Passwords** — Encrypt locally stored credentials with your custom method — only you can decode it.
- **Send Encrypted Messages** — Communicate secretly using an encryption only you and your friend know, even in public group chats.

---

## Getting Started

Visit the live site and choose an encryption method from the home page:

1. Select a **classic cipher** from the list, or
2. Click **"Make your encryption"** to create a custom method.
3. Encrypt or decrypt your message instantly in the browser.
4. Share your custom encryption with friends to communicate privately.
